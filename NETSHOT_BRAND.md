# NetShot — Brand Brief

## What is NetShot?
NetShot is a premium phone mount that clips onto any tennis net post. It holds your smartphone in the perfect position to automatically record your entire game — no tripod, no setup, no hassle.

## The Product
- Machined from aircraft-grade 6061 aluminium
- Lightweight, minimal, precision-engineered
- Clips to the net in under 5 seconds
- Universal fit for any smartphone

## The App
NetShot pairs with a companion app that uses AI to automatically detect rallies, clip highlights, and organise your sessions — so you can review your technique and improve your game without ever touching an editor.

## Core Values
- **Premium** — aluminium, not plastic. Built to last.
- **Effortless** — mount it and forget it
- **Smart** — AI-powered highlight detection
- **Focused** — made for players who want to improve

## Tagline
> Play. Capture. Enjoy.

## Target Sports
- Tennis (available now)
- Padel (coming soon)

## Logo Direction
The logo should feel **minimal, premium, and technical** — think precision engineering meets sport. Could reference the net, a mounting bracket, a lens/camera, or a clean wordmark. Dark backgrounds, metallic or clean white. No rounded bubbly shapes — sharp, confident, modern.
